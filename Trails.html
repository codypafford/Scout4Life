Scout 4 Life is a website intended to be used by outdoor enthusiasts for the purpose of locating the best and most prominent hiking, biking, and running trails near you! You have the option to sort the trails by most prominent or by distance if you are searching using your current location. You also have the option to set the radius in miles in which you want to search for trails.

We are a non-profit hiking organization, that is primarily involved in creating items useful to anyone who wants to get out in the world and explore. We are dedicated to making websites and mobile apps that help outdoor enthusiasts connect, share information and get out into the places where they love to play.
